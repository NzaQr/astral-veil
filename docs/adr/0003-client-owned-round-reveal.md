# Client-owned round reveal

Match authority resolves a round as soon as both players have a committed selection — pot, hands, and `lastResult` update in one step. The round reveal (plays in the order they were committed, then center, then outcome) is a client presentation concern: an immutable sequence snapshotted at kickoff from the before/after states of the resolving commit, exposing a full table frame per beat, advanced by a thin UI scheduler. Match progression (`advanceRound`) stays outside the reveal module and runs only after the outcome beat on non-terminal rounds. We rejected engine-staged resolution because pacing, reduced motion, and “what the player sees” are not match truth.
